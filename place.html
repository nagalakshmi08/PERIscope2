Placements at Andhra University
Andhra University has decided to hire placement officers in order to analyze the job market and provide better job opportunities for students. Previously, Andhra University’s Andhra University College of Engineering for Women (AUCEW) had 100% placements during Andhra University placements 2022, while AU’s Andhra University College of Engineering (AUCoE) had 95% placements.

Salary packages for AUCEW and AUCoE placements in 2022 ranged from INR 13.5 LPA to INR 15 LPA. Leading companies such as Infosys, IBM, Wipro, and Accenture were among the top recruiters at Andhra University in 2022.
